Privacy Policy

Ebill is designed to keep your billing data under your control. The desktop app works offline, the online invoice maker runs in your browser, and we only receive information when you choose to send it through support or email.

Summary

Ebill does not run a hosted billing account for you. We do not store your desktop app invoices, customer list, product list, PDFs, payment records, business settings, or backups on our servers.

This policy explains what happens in the app, on the website, in the browser-based invoice maker, and when you contact us or use third-party services linked from the site.

Desktop app data

The Ebill desktop app is offline-first. Billing records are stored locally on your computer, including invoices, quotations, customers, products, tax settings, company details, logos, payment records, and backup files.

We cannot access that local data unless you intentionally export it and send it to us for support. You are responsible for keeping your device, operating system account, and backup files secure.

Online invoice maker

The online invoice maker is designed to run in your browser. Invoice details you type into the form are used to generate the invoice output in the browser and are not intentionally sent to an Ebill server.

If you export or import JSON files, those files remain under your control. Avoid entering sensitive information on shared or public computers.

Website and downloads

The website may load static assets such as fonts, icons, images, scripts, and release data needed to display pages, downloads, and update information.

The download and updates pages may request public release information from GitHub so the site can show the latest release, files, and download counts. GitHub may receive standard request information such as IP address, browser details, and request time according to its own policies.

Support

If you email us for help, we receive the information you provide. This may include your name, email address, business name, requirements, or technical details.

We use that information to respond to your request, provide support, or investigate issues. Do not send private invoice data unless it is necessary for the support request.

Third-party services

Ebill links to or embeds services operated by other providers, including GitHub, Google Forms, Google Fonts, Font Awesome, YouTube no-cookie embeds, and Buy Me a Coffee. These services are controlled by their respective providers.

Videos are loaded through a privacy-friendly facade where possible, so the YouTube iframe is only inserted after you choose to play the video.

Your choices

  • Keep using the desktop app offline without creating an account.
  • Decide whether to share support files, screenshots, database backups, or exported data.
  • Delete local app data and backups from your own device when you no longer need them.
  • Contact us to request deletion of information you previously sent through email.
